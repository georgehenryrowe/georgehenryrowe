Oh God.

The shattered remenants of a...spaceship? cockpit lay all around you. A cracked window displays what would be a beautiful desktop background: stars, swirling nebula, ringed planets. Below this a toolbar of destruction unfolds.

Mangled steel, shattered glass. What isn't broken is covered in a viscous, dark blood. You examine your body, looking for wounds and trying to avoid looking at everything else. Not my blood, you think. Where am I? What the hell happened here? Whose blood is this?

The uncertainty tumbles and scrabbles with the more pressing questions of the here and now, leaving no room for emotion...yet. You notice a monitor still flickering in the corner, the last vestige of life in this dead room.

Do you want to go over and look at the monitor, or turn round and explore the rest of your strange location?
