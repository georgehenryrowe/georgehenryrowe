You walk away from the blinking of the monitor, through the devestation, towards what you presume must be the exit of the cockpit.

The metal portal at the end is the seemingly the only intact thing in the room. Your weight pressing against it yields nothing.

Disappointed, you start to turn...Is disappointment the first emotion you have felt since waking up? It seems strange that in a scene of horror and unimaginable violence, the disappointment of an steadfast door is what has first piqued inside you.

Pondering this strange malaise in your emotions, you walk toward the flickering monitor in the far corner.
